Weather Watcher
Weather conditions and forecasts worldwide.

Weather Watcher is an Angular 2 application that accesses weather condition and forecast information from World Weather Online.

Weather reports are produced from the closest weather station based on the value of the provided search argument.

Search arguments can consist of a zip code, a city name, or a city name combined with a state, province, or region.

To learn more about the developer API, visit the World Weather Online web site at: https://developer.worldweatheronline.com.
